How We
Work

Three pillars that drive our impact from coastal communities to global policy.

01
Community Cleanups
We organise monthly beach events globally, bringing together volunteers from all backgrounds to make a direct difference on the coastline.
02
Species Protection
Partnering with marine biologists, we monitor endangered species and implement habitat restoration programmes along critical migration corridors.
03
Global Advocacy
We turn field data into policy proposals, engaging governments and corporations to adopt binding commitments on plastic reduction and ocean health.
